Midnight

Roll 6 dice. Hold at least 1 die each roll. You must hold a 1 and a 4 to qualify.

Game Setup

How to Play:

  • You roll 6 dice.
  • You must hold at least 1 die each roll.
  • You must hold a 1 and a 4 to qualify.
  • If you qualify, your score is the sum of the remaining 4 dice.
  • If you do not qualify, your score is 0.
  • Keep rolling until all 6 dice are held.
  • Each player gets one turn.
  • The player with the highest score wins!

Player's Turn
